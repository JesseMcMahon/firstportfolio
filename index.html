How it works.

Hiring a web developer can be confusing. There are so many different languages and types of developers. It can be daunting and seem impossible at times. I strive to make the process as simple and as enjoyable as possible

Have an idea

This could be a business idea, an idea for an app, or anything else you can think of! It can be part of an idea or you can have the whole thing planned out. We will work together to bring that idea to life.

Choose a developer

This part is important. A good client-developer relationship is paramount to creating something both parties can be proud of. We will go over every detail to ensure we both have the same vision moving forward.

Create

This is where the fun starts. I will get to work bringing your dream to life. This will be a collaborative effort as I will be updating you regularly and making changes based on your feedback.

Review and rejoice

Ahh at last! You did it. You now have something that is 100% yours and that is something to be proud of. We will continue to work together to keep your new website updated and fresh.
